🌊 Why Izu?

The Perfect Diving Destination

Discover why Izu Peninsula is one of Japan's best-kept diving secrets.

🚄

Close to Tokyo

Just 90 minutes from central Tokyo by train. Perfect for a day trip or weekend getaway — no flights needed.

🏔️

Natural Dive Site

Dive in natural rocky coastlines and volcanic formations — not artificial reefs. Authentic underwater landscapes await.

🐠

Rich Marine Life

From sea turtles and nudibranchs to seasonal hammerhead sharks. Izu's Kuroshio current brings incredible biodiversity.

🤫

Quiet & Peaceful

Escape the crowds. Enjoy intimate dive sessions in a relaxed, uncrowded environment with personalized attention.
